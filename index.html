Our Services

Comprehensive passive fire protection solutions tailored to your specific needs

Fire Stopping

Sealing openings and joints to prevent the spread of fire and smoke through walls and floors.

  • Penetration sealing for pipes and cables
  • Linear gap sealing
  • Head-of-wall applications

Compartmentation

Creating fire-resistant barriers to contain fires and protect escape routes.

  • Fire-rated walls and partitions
  • Fire doors and frames
  • Smoke barriers

Structural Protection

Protecting structural elements to maintain building integrity during a fire.

  • Steel beam and column protection
  • Concrete protection
  • Fireproof coatings and sprays

Fire Risk Assessments

Comprehensive evaluations of your property's fire safety measures.

  • Full building surveys
  • Compliance reports
  • Remediation recommendations

Training & Consultancy

Educating your team on fire safety best practices and compliance.

  • Fire safety awareness training
  • Passive fire protection seminars
  • Design consultancy

Maintenance & Repair

Ensuring your fire protection systems remain effective over time.

  • Annual inspections
  • Sealant replacement
  • Damage remediation
